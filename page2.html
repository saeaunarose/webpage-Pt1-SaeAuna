Famous South Dakota Trails & Tips

Notable Trails

One of the most popular trails is the Harney Peak Trail (now called Black Elk Peak), which takes you to the highest point east of the Rockies. The views of granite peaks and pine forests are unforgettable.

Another favorite is the Badlands Loop, where dramatic rock formations and colorful canyons make you feel like youre walking on another planet.

Tips for Hikers

  • Start early in the day to avoid heat in the Badlands.
  • Bring binoculars for spotting eagles and bison.
  • Stay on marked trails to protect both yourself and the landscape.
