Hiking Trails in South Dakota

Discover Natures Hidden Gems

Why South Dakota?

South Dakota is known for its wide-open landscapes, stunning rock formations, and historic landmarks. Hiking here gives you the chance to explore everything from peaceful prairies to rugged mountain paths.

Whether youre a beginner looking for short scenic walks or an experienced hiker seeking challenging climbs, South Dakota has trails for everyone.

Top Highlights

  • Mount Rushmore views along nearby trails.
  • Peaceful hikes through the Black Hills National Forest.
  • Unique wildlife spotting, from bison to mountain goats.
  • Scenic trails through Custer State Park.

Planning Your First Hike

  1. Pick a trail that matches your fitness level.
  2. Check the weather before heading out.
  3. Pack water, snacks, and sturdy shoes.
  4. Bring a map or download one offline (there are great apps for this now).
  • Download an offline map app:
    • AllTrails (download trail maps)
    • Gaia GPS (advanced topo maps)
    • Maps.me (free offline navigation)
  • Tell a friend or family member your hiking plan.
  • Start early to avoid running out of daylight.
  • Carry a small first-aid kit just in case.
